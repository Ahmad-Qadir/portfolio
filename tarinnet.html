TarinNet — Multi‑Platform Subscription App

TarinNet is a Flutter app built for Tarin Company to manage internet subscriptions with in‑app recharges, multi‑language support, and a user‑friendly dashboard. It runs across iOS, Android, Huawei, and Linux.
